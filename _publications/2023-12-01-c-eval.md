C-Eval is a multi-level, multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite designed to assess the advanced knowledge and reasoning abilities of foundation models in Chinese. It consists of 13948 multiple-choice questions covering 52 disciplines across four difficulty levels.

Project page: <https://cevalbenchmark.com/>.
