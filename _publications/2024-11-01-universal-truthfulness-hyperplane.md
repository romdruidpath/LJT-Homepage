We investigate whether LLMs encode a universal truthfulness direction inside their hidden representations. We find that such a direction indeed exists and is consistent across layers, models, datasets, and even languages, providing a powerful tool for interpreting and improving LLM truthfulness.

Code repository: [Universal_Truthfulness_Hyperplane](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Universal_Truthfulness_Hyperplane).
